Welcome to Allura Care

Promoting Independence

We are a dedicated home care service designed to help people continue living independently with confidence. We aim to offer you a high quality support service with you having the optimum choice and control for the support you require.

At Allura Care, we provide a range of support services which are tailored for your care and we are able to offer both single and double handed care.

Allura Care works closely with Brent Council and is under strict provisions by the Care Quality Commission to ensure our services meet the national standard.
