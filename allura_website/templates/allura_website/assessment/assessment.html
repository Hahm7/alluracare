Care Assessment

Allura Care's online care assessment tool is designed to provide an indication of the level of care and support you or your family member might need with activities of daily living, based on some simple questions. Activies of daily living represent the key life tasks that people need to manage, in order to live at home and be fully independent.

The assessment tool will guide you through 12 short questions to help determine the level of dependency and provide some useful next steps in exploring care options.

The care assessment gives you a recommendation based on:

Personal Care

Personal care which includes assistance with bathing, dressing, toileting, grooming and support with general tasks to get ready for the day.

Physical Function

Determining how much support you would require to remain mobile around your home. For example, transfers between rooms and using stairs.

Mental Status

Focus on general behaviour, acceptance of support, memory, orientation and communication.
